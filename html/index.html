Cardio Monitoring

Cardio monitoring, short for cardiovascular monitoring, is a medical practice that involves the continuous or periodic assessment of a patient's cardiovascular system, which includes the heart and blood vessels.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is a specialized branch of dentistry that focuses on diagnosing, preventing, and correcting irregularities in the alignment of teeth and jaws. Orthodontists are dental specialists who are trained to treat these issues, which can affect a person's bite, appearance, and overall oral health.

Traumatology

Traumatology is a medical specialty that focuses on the management, treatment, and care of individuals who have sustained traumatic injuries, particularly those related to accidents, falls, violence, and other sudden and unexpected events.

Cardiology

Cardiology is the medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases and conditions related to the cardiovascular system, which includes the heart and blood vessels. Cardiologists are healthcare professionals who specialize in this field and are trained to provide comprehensive care for patients with heart-related issues.

Prostheses

Prostheses (singular: prosthesis) are artificial devices or replacements that are used to replace or augment a missing or non-functional part of the human body. They are designed to restore or enhance the function of a missing or impaired body part, improve the individual's quality of life, and promote mobility and independence.

Pulmonary

Pulmonary is a term related to the lungs and the respiratory system. It is often used to describe conditions, functions, or medical procedures associated with the lungs and the act of breathing. The term "pulmonary" is derived from the Latin word "pulmo," which means lung.
